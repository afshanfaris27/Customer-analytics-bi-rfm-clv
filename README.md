**Customer Analytics Project: RFM Segmentation and CLV analysis**

**Project Overview**
This Project analyzes customer purchasing behaviour using RFM Segmentation and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis on super store retail dataset. 

**Objective of the project** 

     1. Identify high - value customers, 
     2. Detect customers at risk to churn, 
     3. Helps to Understand the revenue contribuiton across customer segments, 
     4. Helps to estimate long term profitability.
    

**Dataset Informations**
The Super Store dataset contains retail transactions from 2015 - 2018, the dataset contains 793 unique count of Customer IDs as each id represents each customer.

The Dataset Link : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitsahoo/sales-forecasting

**Analytical Techniques Used**  

**RFM Segmentation**

Customers are segmented based on three behavioural metrics:

  1. Recency – how recently a customer made a purchase
  2. Frequency – how often the customer makes purchases
  3. Monetary – how much the customer spends

Based on these metrics, customers are classified into segments such as:

  1. Champions
  2. Loyal Customers
  3. Potential Loyalists
  4. Regular Customers
  5. New Customers
  6. At Risk Customers
  7. Lost Customers

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**

Customer Lifetime Value estimates the long-term value generated by a customer.

The CLV model considers:

  1. Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
  2. Customer retention rate
  3. Contribution margin
  4. Discount rate

CLV helps businesses understand customer profitability over time and evaluate the efficiency of customer acquisition.
